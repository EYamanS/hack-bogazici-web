FAQ

  • How do I participate?

    To compete at Hack Bogazici, you must form a team of 2-4 people and fill out a registration form as a team here. Your team should consist of minimum 2 people to be eligable to participate.

  • Who can participate?

    Everyone,
    Undergraduate, Graduate, PhD Students or maximum 3-4 year work experience. If more experienced, do not hesitate to apply. As long as we have room, we will try to accept all of the teams.

  • What will the subject of Hack Boğaziçi?

    Creating a live and demonstrable prototype which can tackle problems about AI, Image recognition, NLP and Machine Learning according to the theme that will be announced before the start!

  • Can I use whatever technology that I like?

    There is no limit on what you can use. Go all in!

  • What are you expecting from us in the end?

    We are expecting to see a working prototype of your idea.

  • Can I change my team on the event day?

    No, you can't.

  • Do we need to stay in the venue during the event?

    Yes.

  • Which language will be used for presentations?

    English

  • How long will my presentation take?

    Every team will have 7 minutes of presentation time and there will be 2 minutes of Q&A.

  • Will every Team make presentation?

    No, only those who made a working prototype will make presentation.

  • Which criteria will you use?

    Throughout the event, the judges and mentors will observe your work, your approach to the problem and your solution and regarding these criteria:
    • How the solution is related to the theme and problem
    • Creativity
    • Usefulness
    • Presentation / Demonstration

  • What we should bring with ourselves?

    We can provide electricity, internet connection, and food. You should bring your own PC and relevant components.

  • Will there be food?

    Yes, we will provide food and drinks :)

  • Can we apply individually?

    No, you have to apply as a team.

  • If I have any questions,?

    devteamcompec@gmail.com or Contact Us
